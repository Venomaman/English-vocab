### 1. colossal  - Large in size.
    syno - gigantic, titanic, tremendous.
    anto - Miniature, diminutive, Trivial, 
           miniscule, minute.
     
### 2. WISTFUL -> showing or envoking a sad longing for an often idealized.
    Syno - nostalgic, Monny, Mawkish, Maudlin,
         somber(sad), gloomy(sad), despondent(sad), 
         dejected(sad),
         melancholic(sad), reminiscent.
    Anto - Cynical, Hard-boiled, ecstatic, elated.

### 3. UTOPIAN -> idealistic but unrealistic; based on an imagined perfect society (aadarshwadi)
     Syno - Quixotic, Starry-eyed, messianic, 
            romantic
     Anto - Pragmatic, Hardheaded, Unsentimental,
            Prudent, Sagacious.
     
### 4. UMBRAGE ->  Offense, irritation or resentment from a perceived insults.
      Syno - Exasperation, dudgeon, indignation, huff
             Resentiment, Pique
      Anto - Satisfaction, Delight, Gratification.
      
### 5. PARTISAN -> Showing strong, often biased support for one side or party. (Pakshpaati)
        Syno - Partial, Prejudiced, Influenced, Parti pris
               Biased, factional, Polarised.
        Anto - Equitable, Disinterested(NEUTRAL), 
               Fair, Objective.
               
### 6. Diatribe -> A forceful, Bitter verbal attack or criticism.
      Syno - Jeremiad, Rant, Tirade, Lambasting
             Sermon, Harangue, castigation, 
      Anto - Eulogy, Tribute, Panegyric, encomium,
             Applaud
      
### 7. Dormant - Temporarily inactive or at rest, but capable of becoming active.
      Syno - Latent, Inoperative, Suspended,
             Quiescent, Inert, Idle
      Anto - Alive, working, vigourous

### 8. CIRCUMVENTION - The act of avoiding a rule, law, or difficulty through clever means.
      Syno - Evasion, skirting, Eschewal, Dodging
      Anto - Submission, Edurance, Toleration, Abidance.

### 9.Inertia - Resistance to change or movement.
     Syno - Laziness, Indolence, SLoth, Loafing
            Languidness, Lathargy
     Anto  - Ambition, Enterprise, Drive, Hustle.


### 10. PROLIFERATION - rapid increase or spread, especially of something undesirable.
     Syno - Expansion, Augmentation, Accretion, Accrual
           Amplification, Amelioration.
    Anto - Loss, Diminution, Abatement, Depltion.
